System and device information

Software and Hardware tracking of site visitors!

We launched another update yesterday, where our users could also get live updates on the machine that their site visitors are using.
This is a powerful detail, as this allows them to know, the software and hardware of their site visitors. While, web and mobile apps
usally run independent of the system on which they are used. Still, it is statistical to get to know the software and
hardware preferences of your customers. Like if you know your users are mostly on android or windows. Then, you could locate your target
niche with more ease now. As, everybody knows wars between segments of people using Android vs ios users and windows vs Mac users vs Linux users.

More linux users, mean you are really targeting the open source community, and more Mac users, you are targeting the market, with more
buying power.

Every little piece of information that you have about your customer is as powerful than anything, about getting to know them. It’s like
when you meet somebody for the first time. The more you know about them, even if it’s just about their favourite football team. You know
where to categorize them. It’s that simple

Hope you guys are enjoying whurthay as much as we love making and growing this.
