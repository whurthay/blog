New markers and trends graph

We recently revamped the design on whurthay!

We recently deployed starred map markers that differentiates between new and returning website visitors. The design and icon for the markers were changed as well, to accomodate a clean user icon, representing a person, accessing your website.

We made our user visits graph prettier as well, now distinguishing between users based on the continent to which they belong to.

The UI is of flat design, to allow to move the browser into full screen mode. We know, you’d need a hit count browser on a giant screen of your office space, and you also need to celeberate important visitor/customer number milestones. We feel you, fellow whurthaiet.

What’s next

Our team is working on rolling on more features in a few days, like browser, device detection. You’d finally get to know, the tiniest details of your user’s system. Like, their operating system, their brower type, version and even their laptop/phone/table model. For example, a detailed review of your visitor’s device details would be if they visited your site using a Huawei laptop, running a Cent OS operating system and much more
